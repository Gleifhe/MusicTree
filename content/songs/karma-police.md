"Karma Police" is a song by Radiohead from their 1997 album *OK Computer*. Written primarily by Thom Yorke, the song is one of the band's most recognizable tracks. Its music video, directed by Jonathan Glazer, is celebrated as one of the greatest music videos ever made.
