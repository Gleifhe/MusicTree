'Heart-Shaped Box' is the lead single from Nirvana's final studio album *In Utero* (1993). Recorded and engineered by Steve Albini at Pachyderm Studio, its video - directed by Anton Corbijn - won the MTV Video Music Award for Best Art Direction.
