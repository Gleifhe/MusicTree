"No One Knows" is the breakthrough single from *Songs for the Deaf* (2002), featuring Dave Grohl's thunderous drumming. Its memorable riff and groove-driven intensity helped push QOTSA into the mainstream and made Grohl's guest appearance one of rock's most celebrated collaborations.
