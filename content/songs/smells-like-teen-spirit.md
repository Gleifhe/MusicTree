"Smells Like Teen Spirit" is a song by Nirvana from the 1991 album *Nevermind*. Written by Kurt Cobain, Krist Novoselic, and Dave Grohl, and produced by Butch Vig, it became the band's biggest hit and is widely considered one of the most influential songs in rock history.
