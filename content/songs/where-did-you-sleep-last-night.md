"Where Did You Sleep Last Night" is a traditional folk song made famous by Leadbelly, performed by Nirvana as the closing track of *MTV Unplugged in New York* (1994). Kurt Cobain's haunting final vocal performance on the song has become one of the most iconic moments in rock history.
