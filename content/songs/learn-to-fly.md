"Learn to Fly" is the lead single from *There Is Nothing Left to Lose* (1999), featuring one of Foo Fighters' most memorable music videos (a comedy involving Dave Grohl in multiple disguises) and showcasing the band's newly melodic, relaxed sound with new guitarist Chris Shiflett.
