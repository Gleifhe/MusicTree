'About a Girl' is one of the more melodic songs on Nirvana's debut album *Bleach* (1989). Kurt Cobain wrote the song after listening to *Meet the Beatles* for three hours straight, and it was a notable departure from the album's heavier sound. It was later performed acoustically on *MTV Unplugged in New York* (1994), becoming one of the most beloved recordings from that session.
