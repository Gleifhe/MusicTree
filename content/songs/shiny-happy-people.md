"Shiny Happy People" is a joyful departure from R.E.M.'s usual sound, from *Out of Time* (1991). Featuring Kate Pierson of the B-52's on guest vocals, its infectious energy made it one of the band's biggest pop hits, though Michael Stipe has been notably ambivalent about the song over the years.
