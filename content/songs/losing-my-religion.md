"Losing My Religion" is a song by R.E.M. from their 1991 album *Out of Time*. An expression of unrequited love, it became the band's biggest hit and features a distinctive mandolin riff played by Peter Buck. The phrase "losing my religion" is a Southern expression for losing one's temper or composure.
