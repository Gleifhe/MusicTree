'Beautiful' is a song by Christina Aguilera from her 2002 album *Stripped*, written and produced entirely by Linda Perry. The song was a massive commercial and critical success, winning the Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance in 2004 and becoming one of the most recognizable pop anthems of the 2000s.
