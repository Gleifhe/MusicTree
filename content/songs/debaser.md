"Debaser" is the opening track from the Pixies' 1989 album *Doolittle*. Written by Black Francis, the song is inspired by the surrealist film *Un Chien Andalou* (1929) by Luis Buñuel and Salvador Dalí. It is considered one of the Pixies' signature songs.
