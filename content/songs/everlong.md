"Everlong" is widely considered Foo Fighters' signature song and one of the greatest rock songs of the 1990s. From *The Colour and the Shape* (1997), Dave Grohl wrote it about his then-girlfriend (now wife) Jordyn Blum. Its blistering guitar work and emotional intensity made it an instant classic.
