'What's Up' (often incorrectly called 'What's Going On') is the debut single by 4 Non Blondes, written entirely by Linda Perry. Released in 1992 on *Bigger, Better, Faster, More!*, it became an international hit in 1993, reaching the top ten in over a dozen countries.
