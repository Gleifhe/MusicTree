"Jeremy" is a song by Pearl Jam from their debut album *Ten* (1991). Written by Eddie Vedder and Stone Gossard, the song addresses the topic of a student who dies by suicide at school. Its music video, directed by Mark Pellington, won four MTV Video Music Awards in 1993.
