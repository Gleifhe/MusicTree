"Where Is My Mind?" is arguably the Pixies' most well-known song, from *Surfer Rosa* (1988). Its ethereal, dreamy melody and Black Francis's surreal lyric (inspired by a scuba diving encounter in the Caribbean) became iconic, most famously used in the closing scene of *Fight Club* (1999).
