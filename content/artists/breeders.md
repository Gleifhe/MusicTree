The Breeders are an American alternative rock band founded in 1989 by Kim Deal of the Pixies and Tanya Donelly of Throwing Muses. After Tanya Donelly left, Kim's twin sister Kelley Deal joined, and the band achieved their commercial peak with *Last Splash* (1993), which went platinum and spawned the hit single "Cannonball."
