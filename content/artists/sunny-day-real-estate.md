Sunny Day Real Estate was an American rock band formed in Seattle, Washington in 1992. Their debut album *Diary* (1994) on Sub Pop Records is widely regarded as one of the most important emo records ever made, influencing countless bands with its emotionally raw songwriting and dynamic post-hardcore sound.
