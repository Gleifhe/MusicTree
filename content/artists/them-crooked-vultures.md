Them Crooked Vultures is a rock supergroup formed in 2009, consisting of Josh Homme (Queens of the Stone Age), Dave Grohl (Foo Fighters/Nirvana), and John Paul Jones (Led Zeppelin). Their self-titled debut album (2009) won a Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance.
