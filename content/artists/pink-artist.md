Pink (born Alecia Beth Moore, September 8, 1979) is an American singer and songwriter known for her powerful voice and edgy pop-rock sound. Her debut album *Can't Take Me Home* (2000) established her as an R&B artist before her second album *Missundaztood* (2001), co-produced by Linda Perry, shifted her toward rock and became one of the best-selling albums of the decade.
