Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington in 1987. The band consisted of vocalist/guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic, with drummer Dave Grohl joining in 1990. Their second album *Nevermind* (1991), featuring the single "Smells Like Teen Spirit," catapulted them to mainstream success and became a defining record of the grunge era and Generation X.
