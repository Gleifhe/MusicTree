4 Non Blondes was an American alternative rock band formed in San Francisco, California in 1989. The band is best known for their 1992 single 'What's Up,' which became an international hit. Lead singer and primary songwriter Linda Perry went on to become one of the most sought-after songwriters and producers in pop music.
