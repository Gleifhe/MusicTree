Garbage is an alternative rock band formed in Madison, Wisconsin in 1993 by record producer Butch Vig and musicians Steve Marker and Duke Erikson. After seeing Scottish singer Shirley Manson on MTV, Vig recruited her as lead vocalist. Their self-titled debut (1995) and *Version 2.0* (1998) established them as one of the defining bands of 1990s alternative rock.
