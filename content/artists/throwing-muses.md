Throwing Muses are an American alternative rock band formed in Newport, Rhode Island in 1983. Fronted by Kristin Hersh, they became the first American band signed to 4AD Records. Their angular, emotionally intense music and Hersh's raw songwriting were influential on the development of alternative and indie rock.
