Frank Black is the solo project of Black Francis (Charles Thompson), frontman and songwriter of the Pixies. After the Pixies' 1993 breakup, he released his self-titled debut solo album in 1993 under the name Frank Black, followed by *Teenager of the Year* (1994), a sprawling 22-track double album showcasing his eclectic songwriting.
