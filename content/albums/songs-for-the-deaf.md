*Songs for the Deaf* is the third studio album by Queens of the Stone Age, released on August 27, 2002. Featuring Dave Grohl on drums and Mark Lanegan on guest vocals, it became the band's mainstream breakthrough, featuring hit singles "No One Knows" and "Go with the Flow."
