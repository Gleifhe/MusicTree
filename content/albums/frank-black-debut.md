*Frank Black* is the debut solo album by Frank Black (Charles Thompson, formerly Black Francis of the Pixies), released on March 23, 1993. Produced by Eric Drew Feldman, it explores eclectic pop and rock songwriting free from the Pixies' dynamic, showcasing Thompson's melodic sensibility.
