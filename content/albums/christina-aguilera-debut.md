*Christina Aguilera* is the self-titled debut studio album by Christina Aguilera, released on August 24, 1999. Produced primarily by Ron Fair, it launched the career of one of pop music's most powerful vocalists, debuting at number one on the Billboard 200 and spawning multiple hit singles.
