*OK Computer* is the third studio album by Radiohead, released on June 16, 1997. Produced by Nigel Godrich and the band, it is frequently cited as one of the greatest albums ever recorded. Its themes of alienation, consumerism, and political commentary proved remarkably prescient for the digital age.
