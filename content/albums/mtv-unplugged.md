*MTV Unplugged in New York* is a live album by Nirvana, recorded November 18, 1993 and released posthumously on November 1, 1994, after Kurt Cobain's death in April 1994. The performance, which featured acoustic instruments and guest musicians, is widely regarded as one of the greatest live performances ever recorded. The set included covers of songs by Leadbelly, David Bowie (The Man Who Sold the World), and the Meat Puppets.
