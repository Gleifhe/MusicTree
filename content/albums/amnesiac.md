*Amnesiac* is the fifth studio album by Radiohead, released on June 5, 2001. Recorded during the same sessions as *Kid A*, it features more vocal-led compositions and jazz influences, including the Humphrey Lyttelton-led New Orleans jazz of the closing track "Life in a Glasshouse."
