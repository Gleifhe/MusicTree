*Garbage* is the self-titled debut studio album by Garbage, released on August 15, 1995. Co-produced by Butch Vig, Steve Marker, and Duke Erikson, it blends alternative rock with electronic elements and Shirley Manson's distinctive vocals, featuring hit singles "Stupid Girl" and "Only Happy When It Rains."
