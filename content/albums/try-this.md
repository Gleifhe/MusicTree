*Try This* is the third studio album by Pink, released on November 11, 2003. Co-written and co-produced by Tim Armstrong of Rancid, it marked a shift toward punk rock and hard rock, showcasing Pink's evolution as an artist. The album won a Grammy Award for Best Female Rock Vocal Performance.
