*GI* is the only studio album by The Germs, released in 1979. Produced by Joan Jett, it is considered one of the greatest American punk albums ever made, capturing Darby Crash's charismatic nihilism and Pat Smear's ferocious guitar work. The album's title is a play on both "gastrointestinal" and the military term.
