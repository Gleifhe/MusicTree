The self-titled debut album by Foo Fighters was recorded entirely by Dave Grohl, who played all the instruments himself. Originally recorded as a demo tape in 1994 shortly after Nirvana's disbandment, it was released on July 4, 1995 via Roswell/Capitol Records. Despite being a largely solo effort, it launched the band's career and demonstrated Grohl's multi-instrumental abilities.
