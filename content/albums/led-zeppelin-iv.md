*Led Zeppelin IV* is the fourth studio album by Led Zeppelin, released on November 8, 1971. Untitled but commonly known as *Led Zeppelin IV* or *Four Symbols*, it features "Stairway to Heaven," widely regarded as one of the greatest rock songs ever recorded. The album exemplifies the band's mastery of both hard rock and acoustic folk.
