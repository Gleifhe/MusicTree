*The Colour and the Shape* is the second studio album by Foo Fighters, released on May 20, 1997. It features the band's classic lineup with Dave Grohl, Nate Mendel, and Taylor Hawkins (who replaced William Goldsmith during recording), and became their commercial breakthrough, containing iconic singles "Everlong," "My Hero," and "Monkey Wrench."
