*Trompe le Monde* is the fourth and final studio album by the Pixies before their original hiatus, released on September 23, 1991. Produced by Gil Norton, it features a harder, more guitar-driven sound and was the first Pixies album on which Kim Deal had minimal songwriting contributions.
