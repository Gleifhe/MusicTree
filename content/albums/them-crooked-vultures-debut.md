*Them Crooked Vultures* is the debut and only studio album by the rock supergroup Them Crooked Vultures, released on November 17, 2009. Featuring Josh Homme (Queens of the Stone Age), Dave Grohl (Foo Fighters/Nirvana), and John Paul Jones (Led Zeppelin), it won the Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance for "New Fang."
