*The Bends* is the second studio album by Radiohead, released on March 13, 1995. Produced by John Leckie with contributions from Nigel Godrich, it marked a significant artistic leap from their debut *Pablo Honey*, establishing Radiohead as a major force in alternative rock with emotionally intense guitar work and Thom Yorke's stunning vocals.
