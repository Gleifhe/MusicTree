*Pod* is the debut studio album by The Breeders, released on May 21, 1990. Produced by Steve Albini, it was formed by Kim Deal (Pixies) and Tanya Donelly (Throwing Muses) as a creative outlet outside their main bands, featuring a cover of the Beatles' "Happiness Is a Warm Gun."
