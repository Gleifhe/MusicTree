John Baldwin, known professionally as John Paul Jones (born January 3, 1946) is a British musician and one of the founding members of Led Zeppelin. A classically trained arranger and bassist, he brought melodic sophistication to the band. In 2009 he joined Dave Grohl and Josh Homme in Them Crooked Vultures.
