Shirley Ann Manson (born August 26, 1966) is a Scottish singer and the lead vocalist of Garbage. Born in Edinburgh, she joined Garbage in 1994 when Butch Vig saw her performing with Angelfish on MTV. Her striking voice and image became central to the band's identity.
