Steve Albini (July 25, 1962 - May 7, 2024) was an American musician, audio engineer, and record producer known for his minimalist, high-fidelity recording approach. He engineered Nirvana's *In Utero* (1993), recording the album in just two weeks at Pachyderm Studio in Minnesota. He also fronted the bands Big Black and Shellac.
