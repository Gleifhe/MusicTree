William Goldsmith (born July 4, 1972) is an American musician best known as the original touring drummer of Foo Fighters and a founding member of Sunny Day Real Estate. He was recruited to join Foo Fighters alongside Nate Mendel in 1995, but departed during the recording of The Colour and the Shape (1997) after creative conflicts with Dave Grohl.
