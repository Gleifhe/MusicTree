John Michael Stipe (born January 4, 1960) is an American singer-songwriter, visual artist, and film producer, best known as the lead vocalist and lyricist of R.E.M. His distinctive voice and often cryptic, stream-of-consciousness lyrics were a hallmark of the band's sound.
