Nicholas Andrew Oliveri (born October 21, 1971) is an American bassist and vocalist who was a key member of Queens of the Stone Age from 1998 to 2004. His raw, aggressive vocal style contrasted with Josh Homme's melodic approach on albums like *Songs for the Deaf*.
