Thomas Edward Yorke (born October 7, 1968) is an English musician and singer-songwriter, best known as the lead vocalist and primary songwriter of Radiohead. His distinctive falsetto voice and experimental approach to melody and rhythm have made him one of the most influential artists in alternative and art rock.
