Peter Lawrence Buck (born December 6, 1956) is an American musician and co-founder of R.E.M. He is known for his jangly, arpeggiated guitar style that became central to the R.E.M. sound. He played on all R.E.M. albums from their debut in 1983 through their breakup in 2011.
