Georg Albert Ruthenberg, known professionally as Pat Smear (born August 5, 1959), is an American musician. He co-founded the punk band The Germs in 1977 before joining Nirvana as a touring guitarist in 1993 during the *In Utero* tour. After Nirvana's dissolution, he became an original member of the Foo Fighters, founded by his former tourmate Dave Grohl.
