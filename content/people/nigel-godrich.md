Nigel Godrich (born February 28, 1971) is an English record producer, musician, and mixer, best known for his long-standing collaboration with Radiohead. Beginning as a studio runner, he co-produced and engineered *The Bends* (1995) and has produced every Radiohead album since *OK Computer* (1997).
