Joshua Michael Homme (born May 17, 1973) is an American musician and founder of Queens of the Stone Age. Raised in the California desert, he co-founded Kyuss before forming QOTSA in 1996. He later collaborated with Dave Grohl and John Paul Jones in Them Crooked Vultures (2009).
