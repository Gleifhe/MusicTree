Franz Stahl (born 1962) is an American musician who replaced Pat Smear as guitarist of Foo Fighters in 1997. He had previously played in the Washington D.C. hardcore band Scream alongside Dave Grohl. He was fired in 1999 and replaced by Chris Shiflett.
