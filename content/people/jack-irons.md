Jack Irons (born July 18, 1962) is an American drummer, one of the founding members of Red Hot Chili Peppers, and later the drummer of Pearl Jam from 1994 to 1998. He was the person who gave Eddie Vedder a demo tape that led to Pearl Jam's formation.
