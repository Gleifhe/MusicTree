Black Francis (born Charles Michael Kittridge Thompson IV on April 6, 1965), also known as Frank Black in his solo career, is an American singer, songwriter, and guitarist, best known as the frontman of the Pixies. His unconventional songwriting and influential "quiet-loud-quiet" dynamic helped define indie and alternative rock.
