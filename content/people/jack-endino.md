Jack Endino (born January 11, 1964) is an American record producer and musician known as the 'Godfather of Grunge.' He produced Nirvana's debut album *Bleach* (1989) on a budget of $606.17 and went on to work with many other Seattle-area bands including Mudhoney and Soundgarden.
